Caretta-Caretta Turtles

Introduction

The Caretta-caretta, also known as the loggerhead sea turtle, is an iconic species found in Zakynthos, Greece. This beautiful island is one of the primary nesting grounds for these magnificent creatures in the Mediterranean.

Habitat

The loggerhead sea turtles are predominantly found in the warm waters of Zakynthos. They prefer the sandy beaches of the island for nesting, where they lay their eggs during the summer months.

Conservation Efforts

Zakynthos has several conservation programs aimed at protecting the Caretta-caretta. Organizations work tirelessly to monitor the beaches, protect the nests, and raise awareness among locals and tourists about the importance of preserving this species.volunteers and researchers monitor nesting activities, safeguard hatchlings, and conduct educational programs to promote conservation awareness.

How You Can Help

Visitors to Zakynthos can contribute to the conservation efforts by following guidelines to avoid disturbing the nesting sites, participating in beach clean-ups, and supporting local conservation organizations.
